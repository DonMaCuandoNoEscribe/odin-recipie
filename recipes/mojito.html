Mojito

A Mojito is a refreshing cocktail that originated in Cuba, known for its combination of mint, lime, and rum flavors, making it perfect for a hot day.

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 oz (60 ml) white rum
    • 1 oz (30 ml) fresh lime juice
    • 2 teaspoons sugar
    • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
    • Sparkling Water
    • Ice cubes
    • Mint sprig and lime wedge (for garnish)
  • Instructions:
    1. In a glass, muddle the mint leaves and sugar together.
    2. Add lime juice and rum, then stir to dissolve the sugar.
    3. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
    4. Top with club soda and stir gently.
    5. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge.
    6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
  • Tips:
    • For extra mint flavor, lightly clap the mint leaves before muddling.
    • Adjust sweetness by adding more or less sugar.
    • Use fresh lime juice for the best flavor.
