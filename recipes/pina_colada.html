Piña Colada

A Piña Colada is a tropical cocktail that originated in Puerto Rico, known for its creamy texture and a delightful blend of pineapple and coconut flavors.

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 oz (60 ml) white rum
    • 1 oz (30 ml) coconut cream
    • 1 oz (30 ml) heavy cream
    • 6 oz (180 ml) fresh pineapple juice
    • 1/2 cup crushed ice
    • Pineapple slice and maraschino cherry (for garnish)
  • Instructions:
    1. In a blender, combine the rum, coconut cream, heavy cream, pineapple juice, and crushed ice.
    2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
    3. Pour the mixture into a chilled glass.
    4. Garnish with a pineapple slice and a maraschino cherry.
    5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
  • Tips:
    • For a lighter version, you can omit the heavy cream and use more pineapple juice.
    • Use fresh pineapple juice for the best flavor.
    • Adjust the sweetness by adding more or less coconut cream.
