Pisco Sour

A Pisco Sour is a classic cocktail originating from Peru, known for its balanced flavors of citrus, sweetness, and a frothy egg white finish.

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 oz (60 ml) Pisco (Peruvian grape brandy)
    • 1 oz (30 ml) fresh lime juice
    • 1/2 oz (15 ml) simple syrup
    • 1 egg white
    • 3 drops of Angostura bitters
    • Ice
  • Instructions:
    1. In a cocktail shaker, combine the Pisco, lime juice, simple syrup, and egg white
    2. Shake the ingredients without ice first (dry shake) for about 10-15 seconds. This helps to emulsify the egg white, creating a frothy texture.
    3. Add ice cubes to the shaker and shake vigorously again for another 15-20 seconds until well chilled.
    4. Strain the mixture into a chilled coupe or rocks glass.
    5. Add 2-3 dashes of Angostura bitters on top of the foam. You can use a toothpick or a small stick to create a simple design with the bitters on the foam.
    6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
  • Tips:
    • Simple Syrup: To make simple syrup, dissolve equal parts sugar and water by heating gently. Cool before using.
    • Lime Juice: Fresh lime juice is essential for the best flavor.
    • Egg White: The egg white gives the cocktail its signature frothy top.
